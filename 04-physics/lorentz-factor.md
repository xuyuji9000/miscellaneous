# Formula

$v$ is current velocity \
$c$ is light speed

$\gamma = \frac{1}{\sqrt{1 - \frac{v^2}{c^2}}}$

# $0.5c$ calculation example

$v=0.5c$ 

$ \frac{v^2}{c^2} 
= \frac{0.5^2c^2}{c^2} 
= \frac{0.5^2}{1} 
= \frac{0.25}{1} 
= \frac{1}{4}
$

$ \sqrt{1 - \frac{v^2}{c^2}} 
= \sqrt{1 - \frac{1}{4}} 
= \sqrt{\frac{3}{4}} 
= \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}
$

$ \frac{1}{\sqrt{1 - \frac{v^2}{c^2}}}  
= \frac{1}{\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}}
= \frac{2}{\sqrt{3}}
\approx \frac{2}{1.732}
\approx 1.1547
$

When $v=0.5c$ , then $\gamma \approx 1.1547$ .
